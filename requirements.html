Online Programme Requirements

  1. Meal Plan:A well-structured diet plan.
  2. Bottle:To stay hydrated.
  3. Fitness Tracker:Monitors your activity levels.
  4. Gym Membership:Access to exercise equipment.
  5. Running Shoes:For cardio workouts.
  6. Resistance Bands:Great for strength training.
  7. Yoga Mat:For flexibility and balance exercises.
  8. Dumbbells:Useful for weightlifting.
  9. Body Weight Scale:To track your progress.
  10. Meal Prep Containers:For portion control.
